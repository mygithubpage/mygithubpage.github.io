Question

Choose a teacher you admire and explain why you admire him or her. Please include specific examples and details in your explanation.

Important Points

After briefly describing the teacher, you could then explain why you admire him or her. If you describe what the teacher did and explain why the action was admirable. If you describe the teacher's personal quality or character trait and give details that provide evidence of it—occasions when you noticed it, the effect it had on you, and so forth.
