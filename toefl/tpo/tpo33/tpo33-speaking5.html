(man) Well, actually,

(man) Um, yeah, I'm afraid I forgot tonight I've got a music rehearsal for the bend I'min.

(man) Yeah, I play keyboards for Little Rock Band here on campus and we've got practice tonight to prepare for a concert we're doing that's coming up. It's really kind of important.

(woman) Yeah?

(man) Yeah, I mean, it's my fault I forgot and I want to do the right thing here since I did say I'd work for you. It's just that some of the songs are new to me and tonight's our last practice before the concert. I don't want to sound bad at the show.

Question

Briefly summarize the problem the speakers are discussing. Then state which of the two solutions from the conversation you would recommend. Explain the reasons for your recommendation.

Sample Response

Well, the man's got a problem that he has a musical rehearsal in his band but he promised the woman to cover her shift in the dining hall at the same time. Accordingly, there're two solutions for the man's problem: first the woman could cancel her plan and go to the night shift by herself, or the man could talk to the band leader to take a leave for covering the woman's shift. Well, I guess, the man should go to his rehearsal and let the woman go to her night shift for two main reasons. Firstly, it's the man's last chance to practice his new songs. If he misses it, he will probably sound bad in the performance and that will negatively affect the team play. Second, I think the woman should understand the man. After all, there will be another play on some other time and the man's work must come first. So I believe the man should choose the first solution. (164 words)
