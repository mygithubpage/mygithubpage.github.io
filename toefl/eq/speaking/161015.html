Which of the following functions of smart phones most beneficial to students? 1. taking photos 2. listening to the music 3. recording lectures.

Sample Response

Well, for me, I think the most beneficial function of a smart phone is to record lectures. As a college student, I have many classes to attend. Besides five compulsory courses, I also have to take four optional courses. But sometimes the professor speaks so fast that I can’t catch up with him very well. Now with my smart phone, I can record lectures and write up notes after class. I don’t need to worry if there’s no time for me to take notes in class, so I can concentrate more on the lecture. Also, the recordings will be useful for review at exam time. Last semester, I recorded all the lectures of my economics course and I reviewed these audio recordings before the exam. Finally I got an A+ on the test. (133 words)

Question

Do you agree or disagree with the following statement: a boss or a manager should not form close friendship with their employees.

Sample Response

I think it depends. If the company is small, and the people were already familiar with each other before they teamed up to work in the company, then a close friendship can be motivational and guarantee efficiency and competitiveness; but if the company is large, like a conglomerate listed in the stock market, in that situation, it is neither possible nor necessary for the boss to keep a close friendship with employees. The boss has too many fish to fry and the employees just need to follow the rules and be professional.

Sample Response

Well, I disagree with the statement. Because managers who try to be friends with their staff run into all sorts of problems. A manager’s job is to evaluate staff’s work, give them feedback and make decisions. If the boss makes friends with a certain employee. Others will think the employee has an unfair advantage at performance review time, thus resentment will start to build up. What’s more, when it comes time for promotions, an employee may have expectations that as a friend, the boss will promote him or her. While the boss should choose the best person for the job, not a friend. This could create tension in and out of the workplace.

Sample Response

Personally, I think a boss should form close friendship with employees. Obviously, a deep friendship encourages team cohesion and staff motivation. When employees get difficulties in their works, managers will offer guidance and support to them. On the other hand, employees will also give sincere suggestions to their managers. Besides, employees and their bosses can learn from each other and seek common progress. For example, my boss is always strict with himself on his work, and he keeps a working log of what to do every day. I think it’s a good way to improve the work efficiency. So I learn the importance of time management and I also keep a working log now. (114 words)
