Question

Some students think it is good to choose the future career before entering the university. Others think it is good to take a few university classes before choosing the future career. Which is better for you and explain.

Sample Response

I think it better to choose the future career before entering the university. First, I think it is worthy to make every effort to take specialized courses and obtain certificates for the future career, by which students are busy learning rather than wasting time in the university. Second, university is the best place to enlarge a person’s vision so that they can get well prepared for future career physically and psychologically. Third, you can do many useful things in the university. You can attend many campus activities and volunteer in many organizations. These are helpful to raise a person’s consciousness of cooperation, in turn, it is a kind of improvement for future career. (113 words)
