Question

Do you agree or disagree with the following statement? One of the best ways to learn about a country’s culture is through watching television shows in that country.

Sample Response

I am absolutely in favor of the statement that the most effective way of learning about a country’s culture is through watching television shows. TV show is a work of art that not only depicts the character’s behaviors and perspectives from all walks of life, also acts as vehicle delivering dramatists’ ideology. The production of TV shows is designated to combine visual elements and sound effects together serving as attraction for viewership which makes the process of learning as an amusing and entertaining approach that lasts for a longer period. In addition, the context and the setting of TV programs are diversified, delivering all verities of messages ranging from romance to science fiction, action to legal procedure which guarantees the audiences’ learning process to be comprehensive and fulfilling. (128 words)
