Question

What kind of reading material, such as novels, magazines, or poetry, do you most like to read in your free time? Explain why you find this kind of reading material interesting.

Important Points

A more developed response might begin "I like to read news magazines because they provide more detailed information about current events than television news shows or newspapers can," and then continue on to explain how the added details in news magazines are interesting or beneficial. Note that raters are only going to consider how well you explain your interest, not make judgments about what you read. It does not matter whether your favorite reading materials are physics journals or comic books!

Sample Response

I love reading English magazines in my spare time, and "Reader's Digest" is my favorite. For one thing, I can find articles of different styles in the same magazine. Some of them are lyrical, some are sentimental, some are sarcastic. Also, I could enjoy the beauty of English language. I am particularly fond of short poems published on the magazine. As the saying goes, simple is elegance. Those beautiful yet concise poems always teach me how to view life positively. Finally, it is so convenient to get the latest issue of my favorite magazine. I can buy it in a bookstore, a supermarket, or even a roadside newspaper kiosk.
