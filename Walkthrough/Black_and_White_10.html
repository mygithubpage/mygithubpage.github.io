B1F

Ryoku of the Seven Sages tells you that Ghetsis is testing you. Fight the grunt waiting for you, then drop through to the next floor.

B2F

Pick up the Max Potion, fight the two grunts, grab the TM30 (Shadow Ball), and drop through the southern patch of quicksand.

B3F

Fight the Plasma grunt, and drop through the quicksand patch to the left to reach B4F. Grab the Sun Stone and the hidden Super Potion, take the stairs back to B2F, and drop through the quicksand to the south to return to B3F. Pick up the Max Revive by the south wall and beat the other grunt. Circle around the quicksand, grab the PP Up and drop through the southwest patch of quicksand.

B4F

Beat the two grunts and drop through the quicksand to the bottom floor.

B5F

When you finally reach the bottom floor, Cheren is right behind you. Alder and Ghetsis are here, but Ghetsis tells you that the Light StoneB / Dark StoneW is not. He tells you to find the other legendary dragon so that you can fight the king, then leaves.

The three of you re-emerge in the Desert Resort. While you're talking with Alder and Cheren, your Xtransceiver rings. It's an urgent call from Professor Juniper, asking you to get to the Nacrene City museum immediately.


Nacrene City

When you reach the museum, Professor Juniper, Cedric Juniper, Bianca, and Alder are waiting for you. The professor says that you are caught up in something serious. Lenora leaves the museum and hands you the Light StoneB / Dark StoneW. Alder asks if you are ready to battle N if anything happens to him, and Lenora remembers that "those two" in Opelucid City are Dragon-type specialists. Fly back to Icirrus City and go northeast to Route 8.


Route 8

Route 8

Route 8 is covered in marshes and water. Constant rain means that the route is always mucky. To the north is the Moor of Icirrus, where a marsh has formed from water collected on the uneven landscape. Battle the Trainers and collect the items as you make your way to the moor.

Once you've gathered all the items, return to Route 8. Go south through the marsh, and turn east when you reach the Trainer Tips sign. Talk to the first Parasol Lady to get TM42 (Facade). Tubeline Bridge

Tubeline Bridge

The Tubeline Bridge is a strong bridge made of steel. Subway trains constantly pass by on the bridge's lower level. On Friday nights, a Biker gang that calls themselves the Black Empoleon appear and take over the bridge. After beating the leader, Jeremy, he renames the gang after your lead Pokémon.

Route 9

Route 9

Route 9 is a short, paved road that cuts through a heavily wooded area. On the north side is Shopping Mall Nine, which boasts the greatest variety of goods in the region. When you leave Tubeline Bridge, an Infielder near the gate throws TM56 (Fling) to you. Check out the department store's wares, then leave east for Opelucid City.
